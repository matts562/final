Reservations

FACILITY RESERVATIONS

Thank you for your interest in renting facilities at the University Recreation and Wellness Complex. The primary purpose of RecWell facilities is to provide recreational and wellness opportunities to our members. We strive to fulfill the Department’s mission to enrich the campus experience and encourage healthy lifestyles.


REGISTERED STUDENT GROUP

To request a RecWell facility reservation, please complete the Rental Interest Form. To ensure consideration, this form should be submitted a minimum of 10 business days prior to the event. This form must be submitted by an officer listed on the SUA website.
After completing the rental interest form and RecWell Event Staff has confirmed the event details, a final reservation must include:
  • Facility Use Agreement
  • Copy of Certificate of Insurance
  • Full payment
